Bookmark Folder Download

To import the bookmark folder:

  1. Download the bookmark folder by clicking the button below.
  2. In Google Chrome, click the three dots in the top-right corner and hover over "Bookmarks".
  3. Now hover your cursor over and click "Bookmarklet Manger".
  4. In the top right corner click the three dots then Select "Import bookmarks" and choose the HTML file you just downloaded.
  5. Now you have the folder and can access it in the imported folder.
  6. If you have trouble finding the HTML file, you can check your recent downloads.
